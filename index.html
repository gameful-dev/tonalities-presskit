About the game

Tonalities is a 2D puzzle game where physics and logic are combined with a procedurally generated ambient sound track.

Position your pieces, find your zen, and enjoy over 50 levels alongside a computationally generated soundscape.

About the developers

Art, programming, testing and UI is by Jared Rigby. Originally from the UK, Jared currently works as a computer science educator and learning technology specialist in Beijing, China.

The music and sound effects for Tonalities were composed by Canadian musician Bronson "Linwood" Metcalfe. His portfolio of work can be found on Soundcloud
