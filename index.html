About the game

Tonalities is a 2D puzzle game where physics and logic are combined with a procedurally generated ambient sound track.

Position your pieces, find your zen, and enjoy over 20 levels alongside a computationally generated soundscape.

About the developers

Tonalities is developed by one man band Jared Rigby (Gameful Developments). Originally from the UK, Jared currently works as a computer science educator and learning technology specialist in Beijing, China.

The music and sound effects for Tonalities were composed by Canadian composer Bronson Metcalfe, currently based in London, Ontario.
